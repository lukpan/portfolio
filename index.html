Building the future of learning, one system at a time

My path to instructional design began in international classrooms spanning three continents—from political philosophy at Universiteit Maastricht to leading a humanities department of 30+ educators in Australia. Co-founding 3 Steps Away, a social justice education enterprise, taught me how to scale learning solutions from concept to market—understanding both educational impact and business sustainability.

At commercetools, I've built learning infrastructure serving thousands of enterprise developers across three continents. By pioneering AI-powered content workflows, I increased team efficiency by 60%—freeing 400+ hours annually to scale our learning ecosystem from 0 to 9 interconnected paths with 40+ modules. This work required partnering with Product, Engineering, and Customer Success leadership to align learning strategy with business objectives.

With a Graduate Certificate in Educational Design from Monash University and hands-on experience building production-ready AI tools, I combine rigorous learning theory with technical execution. I'm now seeking senior instructional design opportunities where I can establish comprehensive impact measurement frameworks—demonstrating how learning programs contribute to business outcomes like time-to-competency, user adoption, and customer retention.
